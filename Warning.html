Anti Microbial Resistance Warning

Antimicrobial resistance (AMR) is one of the most critical global health threats of this century. As bacteria evolve to resist medicines, common infections become harder and sometimes impossible to treat. This leads to longer illnesses, higher medical costs, and increased mortality. Without urgent action to reduce misuse of antibiotics and strengthen surveillance, the world could face a future where minor injuries become deadly. Understanding AMR is essential to preventing its rapid rise and protecting global health.
