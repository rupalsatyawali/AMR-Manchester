Anti Microbial Resistance Meme

Preventing antimicrobial resistance begins with small, consistent actions that protect both individual health and the wider community. Avoid using antibiotics without medical guidance, finish prescriptions exactly as directed, and never pressure doctors for unnecessary medication. Good hygiene, vaccination, and early medical consultation help prevent infections before they require treatment. These habits strengthen the immune system by reducing exposure to resistant bacteria and lowering dependence on antibiotics. By taking responsibility now, we can slow resistance, preserve the effectiveness of medicines, and protect future generations.
